rise to the challenge

qubo is a perspective puzzle game that has been in development since May 2018. Each puzzle involves manipulating a three-dimensional solid into a particular shape by rotating it in two dimensions. Each level's three-dimensional geometry is flattened into two dimensions, allowing movement that seems to defy reason, and forcing you to reimagine the way you move and explore the space around you.

reimagine

qubo asks you to reimagine the three-dimensional world by looking at it from one of 6 two-dimensional views

rediscover

each of qubo's 72 levels has something profoundly different to offer

recreate

qubo will challenge your ability to understand the space around you. are you up for the challenge?
